Latest News Meet The Team

The journey began on January 26th, 2020, with the goal of becoming the most reputed e-sports team in Bangladesh. 1hp has quickly risen through the ranks to become a formidable force in the Apex Legends competitive scene. Our passion for gaming, dedication to teamwork, and relentless pursuit of excellence continue to drive us forward.

With a roster of talented players dedicated to achieving greatness, our mission extends beyond winning. We strive to cultivate a positive and engaging community built on friendship, trust, and the enduring spirit of brotherhood.
